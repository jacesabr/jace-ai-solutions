Safety & control

Autonomous, but never out of your hands

These aren't afterthoughts — they're rules built into the engine, proven in the runs above.

Consent gates

The agent pauses before anything irreversible — a submission, a payment, a message — and waits for a human yes.

Never-fabricate rules

The agent only uses data you gave it — if a form asks something it doesn't know, it stops and asks instead of guessing.

Full audit trail

Every step is logged and screenshotted — you can replay exactly what the agent saw and did on any run.

Isolated sandboxes

Runs happen in disposable cloud desktops (E2B), separated from your machines and destroyed after the job.
