FAQ

Common questions

If yours isn't here, it's a one-minute WhatsApp message away.

My business isn't technical. Can we still use AI?
Yes — most of our automation work is for non-technical teams. If your staff spends hours on email, forms, documents, scheduling, data entry, or answering the same questions, those are exactly the tasks AI handles well. You describe the work in plain language; we handle everything technical.
How much does it cost?
The consultation is free, and no payment happens until you're convinced we can deliver value. Scope sets the price, you'll always know it before we build — and if we don't believe the project will leave you more profitable and less stressed, we'll decline it rather than take your money for a sub-par result.
What if we're not happy with the result?
Then you get your money back. If you feel the product or service isn't up to industry standard, we offer a no-questions-asked refund with no time limit — that guarantee is how we hold ourselves to 100% client satisfaction.
What happens after delivery?
Lifetime support, included. Any tech issue or modification needed to keep the system performing to the standard of its original task is on us — no questions asked. The only thing we'll ask is our motto: "What can we do to make this better?"
How fast can we see something working?
Prototypes are typically a matter of days to a couple of weeks, not months. We deliberately scope the first build small so you can judge real results on your real workflow early.
What about our data and privacy?
Your data stays yours. Where requirements are strict we can deploy models on your own infrastructure (self-hosted, even fully offline) — inference optimization and self-hosted LLM serving are a core specialty, not an afterthought.
Do you only do chatbots?
No — chat is just one interface. We build agents that execute multi-step work, automation pipelines with no chat at all, classic ML (fraud detection, prediction, recommendations), and complete AI-powered products with payments and SEO.
Who actually does the work?
The founder, personally — every intake and every final sign-off — alongside a few handpicked associates with deep domain-specific knowledge. Nothing is handed to Fiverr freelancers, offshore teams, or a project manager juggling ten projects on rotation. That structure is deliberate, and it's also why we can't accept every project.
